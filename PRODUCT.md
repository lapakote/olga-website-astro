# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Three segments, self-selected via the existing БИЗНЕСУ / ДЛЯ СЕБЯ and ЗНАЕТЕ МЕНЯ / НЕ ЗНАЕТЕ МЕНЯ fork in the source content:

- **Top-managers and owners of IT/business companies** — evaluating Olga for scientific advising (CSO-style), strategic sessions, or research before a major decision. Job to be done: confirm she has the academic rigor and business-world fluency to be trusted with a leadership or culture problem.
- **Private coaching clients** — looking for a structured, evidence-based personal upgrade (career, life transition, "luck psychology"). Job to be done: feel this is credible psychological work, not generic life-coaching, and picture themselves in an 8-10 session program.
- **Conference organizers, media, and journalists** — scouting a speaker or expert commentator. Job to be done: quickly find talk topics, past stages, and press mentions to justify a booking or quote request.

All three are time-pressed professionals scanning on desktop or mobile, comparing Olga against both "typical business consultants" and "typical coaches/gurus" — she needs to read as neither.

## Product Purpose

A personal site for Olga Abramova, social and organizational psychologist and anthropologist (HSE, IEA RAS), built to carry three offers — speaking, courses, consulting/advising, coaching — without collapsing into a single generic "book a call" funnel. Success looks like each of the three segments above finding their entry point within the first screen or two and self-routing to the right page, while the site overall reads as scientific authority operating at global scale, not a typical "expert site" in this niche.

## Brand Personality

**Global strategist-visionary.** Scientific weight paired with scale and forward motion: someone who treats any project as a strategic, data-grounded problem viewed through what's happening worldwide in science, business, and technology right now — not a motivational speaker, not a warm life coach.

Visual/structural cues confirmed from reference (machinescanthink.ai): full-bleed video intro on the landing screen (footage exists in `media/`), scroll-driven animation rather than simple fades, and large, confident typography carrying authority instead of decorative elements.

## Anti-references

- **Typical "coach site":** cream/beige tones, handwritten or soft script fonts, beach/lifestyle photography, quotes on gradients. Reads as B2C wellness, undercuts the academic/strategic positioning.
- **Corporate university officialdom:** the stiff, bureaucratic feel of an official HSE/department page. Olga is an HSE-affiliated scientist, but the site must feel like her personal authority, not an institutional page.
- **Typical infobiznes/guru:** loud CTA buttons, countdown timers, "transformation in 3 days" urgency copy. Conflicts with the evidence-based, no-hype positioning explicit in the source text ("научпоп", "данные и статистика", "без иллюзий").

## Design Principles

1. **Data over claims.** Every section earns its authority from credentials, research, or named results, not adjectives — mirrors Olga's own "только данные и проверенные стратегии" stance.
2. **Scale through space, not crowding.** "Global strategist" is expressed with generous contrast and breathing room between blocks, not by packing in every credential and topic at once.
3. **Typography is the authority signal.** Large, bold type carries the brand's weight; decoration stays minimal so the words and data read as confident, not loud.
4. **Let visitors self-route early.** Preserve the БИЗНЕСУ/ДЛЯ СЕБЯ and ЗНАЕТЕ МЕНЯ/НЕ ЗНАЕТЕ МЕНЯ fork from the source content as a real navigational choice near the top, instead of forcing one funnel.
5. **Practice what she teaches.** The build itself (motion timed to attention, structured hierarchy, scroll pacing) should demonstrate the psychological and scientific principles Olga talks about, not just describe them in copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA. Given the heavy reliance on full-bleed video intro and scroll-driven animation, every motion sequence needs a `prefers-reduced-motion` fallback that still conveys the content (crossfade or instant reveal, no motion-gated visibility). Primary audience and copy are Russian-language; no other accessibility constraints specified.
